 WHO  Situation Report 67: 91 203 93 Ireland confirmed 302 new cases, the largest increase to date, and three deaths. This gave a total of 2,121 confirmed cases and 22 deaths. The government issued strict guidelines beginning at midnight where people had to stay at home with specific exceptions, such as essential employees for essential businesses. Notably, residents would be allowed to leave solely for going to the grocery store, pharmacy, for a medical appointment, or for exercise with a maximum 2 160 km (1.2 160 mi) radius from their house where social distancing must be practised and exercise time must be kept short. 91 204 93 Italy confirmed a new highest single-day death toll with 919 deaths (surpassing Spain's total released earlier in the day), increasing the country's total to 9,134. Overall, confirmed cases of infection rose by 5,959 to 86,498. 91 205 93 Spain recorded the highest single-day death toll worldwide to date, with 769 deaths. There were now 64,059 confirmed cases, up from 56,188. 91 206 93 The United States confirmed at least 101,242 cases based on figures collated by  Johns Hopkins University , overtaking China (81,782) and Italy (80,589).
